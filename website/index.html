Spotify Top 200 2020-2021

Intro

First, we will do a presentation of the top artists to get a better idea of the popularity trend. We will pick the 10 artists with the best ranking.

Each of them will be represented by their picture that we will be able to select to show some more information like their rank, their top songs, and the genre of music. We took inspiration from the display that we can find on the Spotify profiles.

To represent the top songs independently of the top artists we will simply display a playlist as it can be found on Spotify with the possibility to listen to each song.
